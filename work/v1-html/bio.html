Biography

My name is Michael Christopher Kilmer. I was born and raised in Worcester Massachusetts, and still currently reside there. I graduated from the Visual Design program at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth in 2008 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in digital media. I am currently interested in Web Development and Design(Flash and Web Standards), 2d Animation, 3d Animation, 3d Modeling, Storyboarding, Game Design, Level Design, and Video Game testing. In my spare time I play guitar, video games, skateboard, watch films and read. My favorite writer is Neil Gaiman, my favorite Director is Quentin Tarantino and my favorite style of music is Indie Rock. Check out my music here at Myspace.
